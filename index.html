Little Lemon
In a world that no longer existed, in a desolate landscape filled with ashes, there was a lemon named Little Lemon. Little Lemon was different from other lemons, as he had a life and consciousness of his own. Before the apocalypse, Little Lemon lived in a garden full of fruits and vegetables, surrounded by people who cared for him and made him grow. But one day, the world changed forever. A devastating nuclear blast leveled the city, leaving only ruins and rubble. Little Lemon miraculously survived the explosion. His thick, tough skin protected him from the radiation and extreme heat. At first, he felt alone and abandoned, but he soon discovered that he had a mission.
Now he needs our help to accomplish this mission.
